Fashion, tech, and the human experience: learn more about the influences that express themselves in my work.

Design is in Vogue.

A lot of people flip quickly through the first ten or so pages of any fashion magazine -- they're filled with ads, after all. For me, these ads were an initial form of design education. I didn't realize it at the time, but I was actually receiving a lesson in layout, typography, and art direction.

Tech & the City

After traveling and living in different places around the globe, I moved to New York to work for a tech startup. While I wasn't in a creative position, this role opened my eyes to the exciting and fast-paced world of tech. I was sure I wanted to stay in the industry, but needed a more creative position.

Ideas to Reality

Soon after finishing a UI/UX full-time bootcamp, I started gaining freelance clients for small branding and interface design projects. After that, I moved onto agency work and was able to get my feet wet in a wide variety of roles and industries.
